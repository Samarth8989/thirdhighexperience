üïâÔ∏è The Path of Hatha Yoga

Hatha Yoga is a gentle, slow-paced practice that focuses on basic postures and breathing techniques. The word "Hatha" comes from Sanskrit: "Ha" meaning sun and "Tha" meaning moon, representing the balance of opposing forces within us.

Our classes emphasize proper alignment, mindful breathing, and holding poses for longer periods to build strength, flexibility, and inner awareness. This practice is perfect for beginners and those seeking deep relaxation and spiritual growth.
